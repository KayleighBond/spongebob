Mr. Krabs
SpongeBob| Sandy Cheeks| Squidward| Plankton| Patrick|

Mr. Krabs is a red crab who lives in an anchor with his daughter, Pearl, who is a whale. He is the owner and founder of the Krusty Krab restaurant as well as the employer of both SpongeBob and Squidward.

Mr. Krab's Quotes
  • "A 5 letter word for happiness: MONEY."
  • "A man works hard all week to keep his pants off all weekend."
  • "Money is the ultimate source of joy."
  • "If I don't make any money today I'll surely break out in a rash!"
