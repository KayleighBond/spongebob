Patrick
SpongeBob| Sandy Cheeks| Squidward| Plankton| Mr. Krabs|

Patrick is the ignorant but very humorous best friend of SpongeBob SquarePants. He is portrayed as being an overweight pink starfish, who serves as the village idiot of the underwater city of Bikini Bottom.
