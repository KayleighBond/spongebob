Sandra "Sandy" Cheeks is a fictional character in the American animated television series SpongeBob SquarePants. She is an anthropomorphic squirrel who wears a diving suit and lives underwater. ... Sandy was created and designed by marine biologist and animator Stephen Hillenburg, the show's creator.
