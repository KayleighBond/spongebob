Plankton
SpongeBob| Sandy Cheeks| Squidward| Patrick| Mr. Krabs|

Plankton is the nemesis and the former best friend of Mr. Krabs. He operates the Chum Bucket, located directly across the street from Krabs' restaurant, the Krusty Krab. The Chum Bucket primarily sells chum (bait consisting of fish parts), which is considered mostly inedible by the other characters.
